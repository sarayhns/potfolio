About Me

My name is Siti Sarah Umairah binti Yuhanis @ Ismail. I was born and raised in Ampang, Selangor. I am a final-year Diploma in New Media Communication and Contentpreneurship (MC111) student at UiTM Alor Gajah in Melaka. I participate in four cores which are animation, games, websites, and social media.

I am really passionate about learning new things as well as outdoor activities. With this, I can get to know myself better and get a purpose where I fit in an environment. Music is one of my favorite hobbies. Playing musical instruments, singing and creating melodies have been my interests since elementary school. Although I am not a professional musician, if given the opportunity to study in more depth and try out the experience in music, I am looking forward to trying and relating it to what I have learned and experienced in the field of media now.

I also really like designing, in 2D and 3D. With what I learned throughout this 4 semester diploma, I’m sure my design has improved. Next, I easily get along with people and understand every aspect of one’s personality, so group work becomes very good because I practice the concept of “communication is key” so that each group member gets information and assignments given equally and excellently. I can adapt to the environment well and can work under pressure. For me, work under stress should be appropriate to a given task and communication is very important when doing a task.

I am looking for an internship and am excited about working in the fields of design and social media because I am confident and interested in those fields. To get in touch, send an email to sarahyhns@gmail.com.
